Our Services

ECU Remapping

Software modifications that remap an automobile's electronic control unit (ECU) improve engine performance. Manufacturers often limit the ECU's fuel, air, and power settings for safety or regulatory reasons. Benefits include increased horsepower, smoother acceleration, and improved fuel efficiency depending on the tuning type selected.

Suspension Upgrades

Suspension upgrades replace or enhance components to improve stability, handling, and comfort. Common upgrades include performance bushings, coilovers, improved shock absorbers, and stronger springs. Benefits include sharper steering response, reduced body roll, and increased overall control.

Exhaust Systems

Performance exhaust systems allow gases to exit more efficiently, resulting in improved power and throttle response. These systems also enhance sound and aesthetics, and are typically made from high-quality stainless steel for durability.

Body Kits

Custom body kits include bumpers, side skirts, spoilers, and diffusers designed to enhance both appearance and aerodynamics. Built from strong materials like fibreglass, polyurethane, or carbon fibre, these kits combine style with functionality.
