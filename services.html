Our Performance Services

From custom software development to comprehensive performance upgrades, FRC Tuned offers a full suite of services to push your vehicle's limits safely and reliably.

ECU & TCU Remapping

Our core service involves custom reprogramming of your Engine Control Unit (ECU) and Transmission Control Unit (TCU). This is a tailored software calibration to optimize fuel, timing, boost pressure, and shifting logic for maximum power, torque, and efficiency while maintaining reliability.

Suspension Enhancements

Upgrading your suspension is crucial for improved handling and driving dynamics. We offer coilovers, lowering springs, and full shock/strut replacements for better cornering and reduced squat/dive. Benefits include sharper steering response, reduced body roll, and increased overall control.

Exhaust Systems

Performance exhaust systems allow gases to exit more efficiently, resulting in improved power and throttle response. These systems also enhance sound and aesthetics, and are typically made from high-quality stainless steel for durability.

Body Kits

Custom body kits include bumpers, side skirts, spoilers, and diffusers designed to enhance both appearance and aerodynamics. Built from strong materials like fibreglass, polyurethane, or carbon fibre, these kits combine style with functionality.
